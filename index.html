No More Meetings

Choose your Perfect Team

Hello Manager-Wannabe. You think you got what it takes to be the next CEO of Twitter? Well then prove it. In NoMoreMeetings, you are going to assemble a team of workers to attempt to try to accomplish 24 different tasks. The goal: to finish all of the tasks as fast as possible by assigning tasks to your team. Be forewarned, depending on the strength you choose, the tasks may take longer and shorter. In addition, the only way to call a meeting is to have a meeting with everyone! That means some tasks might lose all their progress! Good luck and good managing.
